I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Statistics and Data Science, Southern University of Science and Technology, working in Professor Qi-Man Shao (邵启满)'s group.

I received my Ph.D. in Probability and Mathematical Statistics from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before that, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Taishan College, Shandong University.

My research interests are in probability theory and stochastic analysis. My earlier work focused mainly on stochastic (partial) differential equations. More recently, I have become interested in asymptotic theory in probability and statistics, which I am continuing to study.
